# Welcome to LexDraft

We're excited to introduce **LexDraft**, your AI-powered document co-pilot designed to streamline your writing workflow and boost productivity.

## What is LexDraft?

LexDraft is a modern document editor that combines the power of AI with intuitive project management. Whether you're writing technical documentation, blog posts, or business documents, LexDraft adapts to your needs.

## Key Features

### Agentic Drafting

Our AI understands context and helps you draft content faster. Simply describe what you want to write, and LexDraft will generate a draft that you can refine.

### Project Management

Organize your documents into projects. Track changes, manage versions, and collaborate with your team seamlessly.

### Diff & Patch

See exactly what changed in your documents with our advanced diff visualization. Accept or reject changes with confidence.

### Flexible AI Models

Choose between OpenRouter and Ollama for your AI needs. We support a wide range of models to fit your workflow and budget.

## Getting Started

1. Create a new project
2. Start writing or use AI to draft content
3. Organize your files and track changes
4. Export or share your work

We can't wait to see what you create with LexDraft!
